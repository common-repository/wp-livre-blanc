== Description ==

This plugin allows you to present your white paper, manage your files and automatically send it by email.

= Features =

* White paper block creation and description
* Facebook and Twitter integration
* Easily insert your white paper inside your posts or pages with a simple shortcode
* For every white paper, provide the possibility to opt-in to your newsletter
* Generate a list of email registered to your newsletter

== Installation ==

1. Download from wordpress.org and upload it to your `/wp-contents/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Create you white paper from the plugin menu in your dashboard
4. Add the generated shortcode on your page/post.
5. Enjoy
